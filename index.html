Program Description

The Machining and Manufacturing Technology program at Allan Hancock College provides students with the skills and knowledge necessary for a successful career in the manufacturing industry. Through a combination of classroom instruction and hands-on experience, students learn to operate and program CNC machines, utilize CAD/CAM software, and develop a strong understanding of manufacturing processes. The program emphasizes safety, precision, and effective communication within a manufacturing environment. Graduates are prepared for positions such as CNC operators, machine operators, and CAD/CAM technicians.
