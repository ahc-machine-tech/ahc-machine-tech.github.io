Introduction ================================== To be able to get started with the different projects in this class you will have to create a student account for Fusion 360. This lab explains the steps to achieve that. 1 - Creating an Education Fusion 360 Account ================================== - **Go to the following link** <a href="https://www.autodesk.com/education/edu-software/fusion">Fusiono 360 for Education</a> - **Click on "Download Autodesk Fusion"** <img src="resources/lab0/4.png" alt="Download Fusion Button" width="600"/> - **Under the "All Products" section click the "Select" button for Fusion** <img src="resources/lab0/5.png" alt="Select Fusion" width="600"/> - **Select the "Student" option.** <img src="resources/lab0/6.png" alt="Student Option" width="600"/> - **Click Continue** <img src="resources/lab0/7.png" alt="Download Fusion Button" width="400"/> - **Fill out the form and click "Submit" (Make sure you use your Student email instead of your personal!)** <img src="resources/lab0/8.png" alt="Download Fusion Button" width="600"/> 2 - Open Fusion 360 ================================== Once the form has been submitted and the account created. [[y! Before you start! Make sure you have downloaded and installed Fusion 360 on your computer.
